▶ How to Play
1
🌟 Pick your subjects
Tap subjects to toggle on or off. Gold = selected! Mix them all or focus on one.
2
🐢 Choose your speed
Slow gives you 35 seconds, Medium gives 20, Fast gives 12. Pick what feels right!
3
⚡ Use your power-ups wisely
You get 3 power-ups per game: 50/50 removes 2 wrong answers, Extra Time adds 10 seconds, Skip jumps to the next question.
4
⭐ Earn XP and climb ranks
Every game earns XP. Collect enough to go from Cadet all the way to Einstein! Unlock trophies along the way.
5
🎉 Do the Daily Challenge
A special bonus game every day worth extra XP. Come back each day to keep your streak going!
💡 Top Tip: A right answer beats a fast wrong one. Read carefully, then answer quickly for maximum stars!
