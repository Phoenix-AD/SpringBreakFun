🚀 How to Play
Read the rules then get blasting!
1
🌟 Pick your subjects
On the home screen, tap any subject to turn it on or off. Gold means selected! You can mix all 10 subjects or focus on just one.
2
🐢 Choose your speed
Chill Mode gives you 35 seconds per question. Adventure gives 20 seconds. Turbo Brain gives just 12 seconds. The faster you answer, the more bonus stars you earn!
3
⏱ Watch the timer!
The bar at the top counts down. Green means plenty of time, orange means hurry up, red means almost out! If time runs out the question is skipped.
4
⭐ Earn stars and streaks
Every correct answer earns 10 base stars plus a time bonus. Answer 3 or more in a row to trigger a STREAK BONUS! The game gets harder as you improve.
5
✂ Use your 3 power-ups
Each game gives you one of each: 50/50 removes two wrong answers, +10 sec adds time to the clock, Skip jumps to the next question. Use them wisely!
6
📖 Stories need more time
When a story appears in a pink box, read it carefully first THEN answer the question. Story questions automatically get 3 full minutes so there is no rush.
7
🏆 Collect trophies and climb ranks
Every game earns XP. Collect enough to climb from Cadet all the way up to Einstein! Unlock 12 special trophies along the way. Check the Trophies tab to see your progress.
💡 Top Tip: A correct answer is always better than a fast wrong one. Read the question carefully, then answer as quickly as you can!
